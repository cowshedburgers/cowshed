about us

The Cowshed is a welcoming, independent venue at the heart of the Scarborough community, known for its relaxed atmosphere, friendly service, and quality food and drink. We pride ourselves on creating a space where locals and visitors alike can unwind, enjoy great company, and feel right at home. We combine fresh locally sourced ingredients and create burgers to remember.

got beef?

Sourced and handled from North Yorkshire farms, our beef is pressed and minced in house by our very own team ensuring each patty gets the care it deserves. Great tasting flavours throughout every burger.

best british burger nominee 2025

The Cowshed, Scarborough has been nominated for the Best British Burger Award 2025 - a recognition of their dedication to quality British produce, expertly prepared beef, and flavour-led creativity.

We are thrilled to have been nominated for the British Burger Awards 2025, where the nation's top independent burger makers are recognised across the UK. The Cowshed Burgers in Scarborough was honoured with an Outstanding Achievement award in the Yorkishire category, standing alongside standout nominees and winners from across the country.
